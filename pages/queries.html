Frequently asked questions

These are the most common asked questions about BagBag. Can't find what you are looking for? Chat to our friendly team!

Order & Shipping:

Q.1: How long does shipping take?

Ans. Orders are typically delivered within 3-7 business days, depending on your location.

Q.2: Do you offer international shipping?

Ans. Yes, we ship worldwide! ipping fees and delivery time vary by country.

Q.3: How do I track my order?

Ans. Once your order is shipped, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. You can also track it through your account dashboard.

Q.4: Can I change or cancel my order?

Ans. You can request changes or cancellations within 12 hours of placing your order by contacting us through the Contact Us page.

Payments & Refunds:

Q.5: What payment methods do you accept?

Ans. We accept Visa, MasterCard, PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay.

Q.6: Is it safe to shop on BagBag?

Ans. Absolutely. Our website uses SSL encryption to ensure your personal and payment information is secure.

Q.7: What is your return policy?

Ans. We offer free returns within 14 days of delivery. Items must be unused and in original packaging

Q.8: How do I request a refund?

Ans. Please email us at support@bagbag.com OR send us a message though Contact Us with your order number and reason for return (optional). Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days.

Product & Availability:

Q.9: Are all bags on your site in stock?

Ans. Most products are in stock, but availability can vary. Out-of-stock items are marked and restocked regularly

Q.10:Do you sell authentic branded bags?

Ans. Yes, all our branded bags are 100% authentic and sourced directly from verified distributors.

Q.11: Can I request a custom bag?

Ans. Currently, we do not offer customizations, but stay tuned — it’s something we’re exploring!

Men’s & Women’s Collections:

Q.12: Are the men’s and women’s bags different in quality?

Ans. No — all our bags, regardless of collection, meet the same high standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Q.13: Can I filter bags by style or purpose?

Ans. Yes, use our dropdown tool to browse through types of bags.

Account & Support:

Q.14: Do i need an account to shop on BagBag?

Ans. No, and currently you are not able to make an account on BagBag. We hope to bring that facility soon!

Q.15: How can i contact customer support??

Ans. Visit our Contact Us page or email us directly at support@bagbag.com.
